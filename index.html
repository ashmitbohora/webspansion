Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions
What is Webspansion? +

Webspansion is a free service that provides website development for small businesses, nonprofits, and individuals, helping them expand their online presence at no cost.

Is Webspansion run solo? Why use the word 'we'?+

Yes, Webspansion is entirely run by me, Ashmit Bohora, as a solo project. I use the word 'we' to reflect the professionalism and advertising technique behind the mission to create free websites for businesses, nonprofits, and anyone that needs one!"

What is Project 100?+

Project 100 is my mission to create 100 free websites for small businesses, nonprofits, and anyone in need. It's my way of giving back and helping others build their online presence without any cost!"

How does Webspansion offer free websites? +

Webspansion is a passion project aimed at helping communities grow online. We don't charge for website creation but rely on donations to cover costs and allow us to continue offering free services.

Who is eligible for a free website?+

Any small business, nonprofit, or individual looking to enhance their online presence can request a free website from us.

Why are your services free? +

Our mission is to help people and organizations grow online without financial barriers. We offer our services for free to give back to the community and rely on donations to sustain the project.

Can you redesign an existing website?+

Absolutely! We can take your existing site and give it a fresh look while preserving your content.

Do you build mobile-responsive websites?+

Yes, all of our websites are mobile-friendly and fully responsive, meaning they will look great on any device, from smartphones to desktops.

More Questions
How can I support Webspansion?+

You can support us by making a donation through our Ko-fi page. 100% of donations go toward sustaining Webspansion and allowing us to continue offering free services.

What happens with donations made to Webspansion?+

All donations are used to cover the costs of domains, hosting, and expanding our web development services to more clients. Your contributions help us make a bigger impact.

Is there a way to help if I can't donate money?+

Yes, you can support us by spreading the word about Webspansion, following us on social media, or volunteering your skills if you have experience in web development or design.

Can you help me update my website in the future? +

Yes, we're always here to help. Whether you need content updates or technical assistance, just reach out.

What if I encounter technical issues after my website is built?+

If you experience technical problems with the website, simply contact us, and we'll help resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Can I make changes to my website myself?+

Yes, we can guide you through basic updates, and you'll have full access to the content management system to make changes yourself.
